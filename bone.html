Something is lurking in the graveyard...


Choose between different bones to create either your latest nightmare or a new friend


Note: File Path for screenshots in the PC build is in the Bone To Pick_Data folder, which is in the same place as the executable for the game

Note 2: If you play through the itch.io app, you can find screenshots in the place you installed to. By default, on PC it can be found at 

            Users/user_name/AppData/Roaming/itch/apps/bone-to-pick/PCBuilds/Bone To Pick_Data
