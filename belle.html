Hi Twitter! This is Belle. She lives in her account! To pet her, like any of her tweets. You can also play with her by retweeting her tweets! pic.twitter.com/eFG3tBL9DG

— Belle the Cat Bot (@bellethecatbot) September 19, 2019

Belle the Cat Bot is an interactive Tomagotchi-like Twitter bot. By retweeting or liking tweets, users can either pet or play with the bot. Features original ASCII art and a NodeJS bot application hosted on Heroku, all developed by myself.

Bot has been inactive since 2022.

Year: 2019

Development Time: one week
