Hi Twitter! This is Belle. She lives in her account! To pet her, like any of her tweets. You can also play with her by retweeting her tweets! pic.twitter.com/eFG3tBL9DG

— Belle the Cat Bot (@bellethecatbot) September 19, 2019

Done as a rapid prototyping exercise within one week, Belle the Cat Bot is a Twitter Bot that functions as a Tamagotchi-like virtual pet. Users can like tweets from the accounts to pet the account or retweet to play with the cat.
