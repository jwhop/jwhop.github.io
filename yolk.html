YOLK'S ON YOU

low-tech alt ctrl at home game made for the A MAZE AT HOME alt ctrl jam!

To play, you need to slide your mouse across a long surface (maybe the floor?) to crack the egg. Make sure it doesn't break on you though! 

Note: There may be some issues with capturing the cursor on the web build!
