YOLK'S ON YOU

Yolk's On You is a low-tech alt -controller mouse game developed for A MAZE's "A MAZE @ Home - Alt.Ctrl Online Game Jam". The project was developed by myself over approximately 24 hours. In this two player game, players take turns sliding a mouse across the floor to crack an egg, trying not to be the one to break it open.

Year: 2021

Medium: Software, Mouse
