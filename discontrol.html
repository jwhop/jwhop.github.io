Disco[ntrol]
back

Disco[ntrol] is an ongoing collection of minigames paired with a custom-made spherical controller. The controller uses a disco ball light, arcade buttons, and an arduino mega to interface with Unity. Its games are prototypes for now, I continue to try and find game ideas that are 'spherical' :^)

Year: 2022- present

Development Time: started as school assignment, then occasional of part time work

Medium: Arduino Mega, Arcade Buttons, Digital Game
