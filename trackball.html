About

I made this game for the A Game By Its Cover 2020 Game Jam in about four weeks. Being a big fan of alternative controllers, seeing the cartridge in the Famicase exhibition sparked my interest in designing an alt control game playable by anyone without the specific hardware. 

This game is released! Happy to make more levels if there's any interest.  Any feedback is greatly appreciated! For more alt controllers, feel free to follow me on Twitter!
