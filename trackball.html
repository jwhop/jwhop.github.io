Trackball Driver Installation Disk
back

Trackball Driver Installation Disk is a low-tech alt-controller rolling ball game developed for the "A Game By Its Cover" Game Jam in 2019. In order to play, players must turn their keyboard upside down and roll it over a ball. Project was developed over about four weeks. Featured at Indie Makers Syndicate's Makers Play 2021. *Note: While all development on the game is my work, the game was developed using concept art from Mushbuh's Famicase 2020 entry.

Year: 2020

Medium: Software, Keyboard, Tennis Ball
