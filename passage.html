Passage
back

"Passage" is a complete transformation of the 1993 PC game "DOOM". The game features an original controller, textures, and sprites. Players walk around in a Target retail store with nothing but a tape recorder and deer roaming around the store. Project was developed over roughly a month. Note: music, tape recorder sprites, music note sprites, and some deer sprites are not my work

Year: 2017

Medium: Software, Electronics, Tape Recorder
